===== [Heading2] 6. Expected Outcomes =====
Expected Findings:
The Synergy Delta: We expect to demonstrate that GenAI-generated suites provide superior horizontal functional coverage, while human-authored suites provide superior depth in boundary logic, confirming that their combination yields a fault detection rate higher than any single source[cite: 18, 5].
Optimization Efficiency: The study is expected to prove that a multi-stage prompt-chaining pipeline can reduce test suite redundancy by at least 13-20% compared to standard single-shot prompting[cite: 5, 15].
Expected System/Model Performance:
Reduction in Script Flakiness: By providing the model with pruned HTML DOM context alongside functional requirements, we anticipate a measurable increase in the successful execution rate (Pass@1) of automated black-box scripts[cite: 2, 12].
High Functional Validity: We aim to achieve a functional validity rate exceeding 85% for all generated scenarios, identifying complex kịch bản that senior human testers often overlook due to time constraints[cite: 5, 13].
Anticipated Contributions:
Comparative Roadmap: A detailed performance matrix that maps the specific strengths and weaknesses of GenAI vs. Humans in Black-Box system testing.
Integrated Framework: A validated methodology for integrating functional requirements with UI structural data to produce executable and reliable automated test suites[cite: 2, 19].
Practitioner-Informed Guidelines: A set of best practices for QA teams to safely adopt GenAI, focusing on prompt engineering patterns that minimize hallucinations in critical system logic.
